## Diagnosis Rules

If impressions are high but profile visits are low:

- Hook is working less as identity signal.
- Add a clearer viewpoint or topic ownership in posts.

If profile visits are high but follows are low:

- Improve bio and pinned post.
- Make recent posts visually consistent with the account promise.

If likes are high but reposts/bookmarks are low:

- Add more frameworks, tables, checklists, and "how to judge" posts.
- Create save-worthy AI full-stack radar posts.

If replies drive most impressions:

- Keep replying, but add daily main posts to capture and convert the attention.

If meme replies outperform analysis:

- Do not abandon analysis. Use meme replies as entry points, then post a deeper follow-up.
