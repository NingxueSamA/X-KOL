# Content Patterns

## Content Mix

Target weekly mix:

- Short opinion posts: 12-20 per week
- Data observation posts: 5-8 per week
- Threads: 2-3 per week
- Quote posts: 5-10 per week
- Light reactions/memes: 3-6 per week
- Weekly review: 1 per week

Balance:

- AI full-stack: 35%
- Web3/crypto and on-chain narratives: 30%
- A-share/H-share/macro liquidity: 20%
- Trend methodology and review: 15%

## Pattern 1: 细节信号帖

Use when a speech, video, meeting, financial event, or product launch contains a subtle signal.

Structure:

```text
[事件]，真正值得看的细节是：

[一个具体细节]

很多人以为这是 [表层解释]。

但重点在后面：

[关键句 / 关键动作 / 关键名单 / 关键数据]

看到这里其实已经很明显了：

[你的判断]
```

Example source behavior:

- Trump China visit speech.
- The hidden signal was not the polite language, but "I brought America's greatest entrepreneurs."

Use for:

- AI company launches
- NVIDIA / AMD / HBM earnings
- US-China tech policy
- Crypto project roadmaps
- ETF/regulatory/institutional entry news

## Pattern 2: 权力排序帖

Use when the signal is visual: standing position, arrival order, seating, camera time, who is present or absent.

Structure:

```text
[事件] 里，真正值得看的不是谁说了什么。

而是谁站在哪里，谁第几个出现，谁被镜头给了时间。

很多人会觉得这是 [礼宾 / 流程 / 巧合]。

但在 [政治 / 资本 / 科技] 里，排序从来不是随机的。

[关键排序 / 关键名单 / 关键座位]

这说明：

[你的判断]
```

Example source behavior:

- Air Force One arrival in Beijing; Musk was the fourth person down the stairs.
- Company list: NVIDIA, Tesla, Qualcomm, Goldman.

## Pattern 3: 时代气味帖

Use when an ordinary news item signals a broader cycle change.

Structure:

```text
[新闻]，很多人当成普通新闻看。

但我觉得，这背后有一种时代气味。

上一轮周期里，[旧结构]。

而现在，[新变化]。

因为 [行业/时代] 真正重要的东西变了：

不是 [旧变量]。
不是 [旧变量]。
而是 [新变量]。

所以 [你的判断]。
```

Example source behavior:

- Luo Yonghao returning to X as a sign of Chinese tech people reconnecting with global information flow.

Use for:

- Chinese AI founders going global
- Web2 people entering AI/crypto
- X becoming a real-time cockpit for global tech and finance
- Old internet energy returning with AI as the new protagonist

## Pattern 4: 轻反应 / 梗帖

Use for speed and personality, not authority.

Rules:

- Keep to 10-15% of content.
- Best when a fresh meme, guest list, founder drama, or "missed the train" market moment appears.
- After a light reaction gets reach, follow with a deeper detail-signal or power-order post.

## Pattern 5: AI 全链雷达

Use to anchor the account's main expertise.

Track:

- Storage
- HBM
- GPU/compute
- Agents
- AI x Crypto
- Model cost and productization
- NVIDIA, AMD, Micron, TSMC, Broadcom, cloud vendors

Prompt shape:

```text
大冥灯 AI 全链雷达：

今天我会盯三个变量：

1. [资金/价格/订单]
2. [产业链/产品/技术]
3. [市场情绪/叙事变化]

我现在的判断是：
[short judgment]

错了回来复盘。
```
